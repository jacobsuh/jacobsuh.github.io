MESSY MIX 01/16: January Jewels

A messy mix of the best songs I found in the past month. Saw Anna Lunoe for the first time when she opened for Dillon Francis's IDGAFOS Weekend in L.A. (she's fantastic), so most of these songs are either from her or one of her mixes. I'm going to try making one of these a month, but I'm going to keep it pretty open-ended. Enjoy the first ever, January episode of Messy Mixes.

Aired live on WXTJ Charlottesville 100.1 FM on Jan. 24, 2016.
