MESSY MIX 07/16: Tiny Room House

Everything but god-awful big room house. I've been listening to a lot of deep house and French house lately, so here's some of my favorites from the ones I listened to in the last month. It's kind of a departure from my previous mixes, but it feels good to go back to what I used to listen to exclusively before DJing for radio.

Aired live on WXTJ Charlottesville 100.1 FM on July 1, 2016.
